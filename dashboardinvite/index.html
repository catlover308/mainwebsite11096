FRC Team 11096
Student Registration

1. Parent creates a FIRST account

A parent or guardian must register at my.firstinspires.org.

2. Add your student

From the dashboard, go to Parent/Guardian then Youth, and select Add a Youth. Complete the registration flow for your student.

3. Apply to Team 11096

During the flow, you will be prompted to join a team. Enter team number 11096 and submit.

4. Complete consent and release forms

Complete any remaining consent and release forms. Both the parent and student portions must be signed before the student can attend competition.
